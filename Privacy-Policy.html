Privacy Policy

A Privacy Policy is a legal document that outlines how a website or app collects, uses, stores, and protects users' personal information. It typically includes details about the types of data collected (such as name, email, IP address), how the data is used (for example, to improve user experience, send promotional offers), and how users can control their information (like opting out of marketing emails or deleting their account). In essence, a Privacy Policy serves to inform users about their rights regarding their personal data and helps build trust by demonstrating transparency and compliance with privacy laws. It's essential to update the Privacy Policy regularly, especially when there are changes in data practices or legal requirements. Failure to have a compliant Privacy Policy can lead to legal consequences and damage to the reputation of the website or app.

1. Personal Data we collect

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us when using our Website. This may include information such as your name, email address, mobile number, location, and any other details necessary for property searching and contact purposes. We may also automatically collect certain information about your device and usage of our Website, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and interactions with our content.

2.How We Use Your Personal Data

We use the personal data you provide to deliver the services you request, such as property listings, contact inquiries, and account management. Additionally, we may use your information to communicate with you about our services, personalize your experience, and improve our Website's functionality and user experience. We do not use your personal data for any purposes other than those outlined in this Privacy Policy without your consent.

3.Who We Share Your Personal Data With

We may share your personal data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our Website, processing transactions, or providing services on our behalf. These service providers are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information and are only permitted to use your personal data as necessary to provide the services requested by us.

4.Data Storage and Retention

We store and retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. We take reasonable measures to ensure the security of your personal data during storage and transmission, including encryption and access controls.

5.Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or update your personal data held by us. You may also request deletion of your information, subject to legal obligations. Additionally, you have the right to opt-out of receiving promotional communications from us. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided in this Privacy Policy.

6. Data Protection Practices

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include encryption, access controls, regular security audits, and employee training on data protection best practices. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal data.

7. Third-Party Websites, Apps, and Services

Our Website may contain links to third-party websites, apps, or services that are not operated by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit to understand how they collect, use, and disclose your information.

8.Children

Our Website is not directed towards children under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child, please contact us immediately, and we will take appropriate steps to remove the information from our records.

9.Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on our Website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your personal data.

10. How to Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or grievances regarding our Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal data, please contact us support@shifthoja.com . We take privacy seriously and will do our best to address any issues or concerns you may have. By using our website, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal data as described in this Privacy Policy.
